Curated guest shifts & international events

Core

International guest shifts, festivals, bar takeovers and masterclasses — curated case by case.

More information

What it is

Guest shifts and hospitality events involving international bartenders, designed to create meaningful collaboration rather than one-off appearances.

Why curation matters

Not every bartender fits every bar. We focus on cultural alignment, audience relevance, and context — not random availability.

How Bartendly is used

Events are structured and visible inside the Bartendly platform through bartender profiles and event listings, creating visibility beyond the night itself.

Who executes

Independent bartenders and hosts. World of Mixologists curates and connects — it does not employ talent.

Sense & Soul — by Fernando Fastuca

Partner

A signature food & cocktail experience built around narrative, pairing and human connection.

More information

What Sense & Soul is

Sense & Soul is a defined hospitality experience combining gastronomy, cocktails, and storytelling. It is not generic consulting and not menu engineering — it is an experience delivered in person.

Who it’s for

Premium venues and brands seeking a narrative-driven format and a high-touch guest experience.

Who does what

Fernando Fastuca creates and delivers the experience. World of Mixologists curates, positions and connects. Bartendly supports the commercial and operational flow.

Sense & Soul is conceived, owned and led by Fernando Fastuca.

Curated formats & tours (BBB)

Selective

Multi-city formats combining guest shifts, travel and impact-driven initiatives.

More information

What this is

Bartenders Beyond Borders (BBB) is a curated format developed in partnership with Tony Pescatori, combining guest shifts, travel, and impact-driven initiatives.

When it makes sense

Long-term brand storytelling, multi-city activations, or projects where community and impact are part of the objective.

Important clarity

BBB-style formats are activated selectively when aligned with the project — they are not the default offer.

What is World of Mixologists

A premium editorial platform for the people, places and moments that make hospitality worth caring about.

World of Mixologists exists to document, highlight and support what actually matters in hospitality: bartenders, bars, creators, guest shifts, concepts, cities and experiences that create real desire, memory and return value.

We document

A living global view of cocktail culture

WØM captures bars, guest shifts, creators, hospitality scenes and city energy as a curated editorial layer — not noise, not a database without soul, but a point of view.

We highlight

Real places. Real people. Real experiences.

We highlight bars and professionals because they create something worth feeling, remembering and revisiting — not because they bought visibility or fit a mechanical scoring system.

Why it exists

The hospitality world deserves visibility that feels human, credible and desirable. Too much attention is captured by systems that are paid, artificial, limited or disconnected from the emotional reality of going out. WØM exists to show the bars, the people and the moments that create true hospitality value.

What we value

Innovation, creativity, generosity, atmosphere, fun, social energy, strong identity, memorable hospitality and places where people genuinely enjoy their time. We show what actually matters in hospitality — the people, the places and the moments that make you come back.

What makes us different

Not a ranking. Not a paid badge. Not an artificial authority machine.

World of Mixologists exists alongside the broader cocktail ecosystem, but it stands on a fundamentally different philosophy: not rank, not scarcity for prestige, not paid legitimacy — but relevance, desirability and return value.

Our difference is structural

—Not ranked. Hospitality is not reduced to a rigid ladder.
—Not limited in number. Great places are not valid only because they fit a quota.
—Not paid visibility. Editorial legitimacy is not for sale.
—Not algorithm-driven. Hospitality is human, emotional and social before it is measurable.
—Not only about perfect luxury codes. Some places matter because they feel alive, generous, magnetic and unforgettable.
We do not ask only whether a place looks right. We ask whether it makes people want to return.

That is the logic behind the WØM Selection: a badge designed to coexist with ecosystem markers like 50 Best or Top 500 while standing for something else entirely — atmosphere, feeling, fun, people, experience, memory, social energy and desire.

The WØM Selection

The WØM Selection is for bars worth discovering and worth returning to. It can exist alongside known industry badges, but it is built on a different foundation.

It is not ranked. It is not capped. It is not bought. It is not limited to bars that simply tick expected boxes. It is about places where atmosphere, people, experience and memory create a genuine reason to come back.

A place you don’t just visit — a place you come back to.

What the badge can stand for

Atmosphere Feeling Fun People Experience Memory Social energy Desire to return

The goal is not to imitate existing authority systems. The goal is to highlight bars that genuinely matter to the people who live, work and go out in this world. Some are elegant. Some are wild. Some are iconic. Some are deeply local. What matters is that they create return value.

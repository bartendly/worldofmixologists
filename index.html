What is World of Mixologists?

An editorial voice for the people, places and moments that make cocktail culture worth following.

World of Mixologists documents, highlights and supports what actually matters in hospitality: bartenders, bars, creators, guest shifts, concepts, cities and experiences that create desire, memory and return value.

We reveal

A living view of cocktail culture

WØM captures bars, guest shifts, creators, hospitality scenes and city energy as a curated editorial layer — not noise, not a generic database, but a point of view.

We amplify

Real places. Real people. Real events.

WØM gives visibility to the bars, professionals and moments that create true hospitality value — while Bartendly turns that visibility into structured discovery and opportunity.

WØM Selection

Independent. Not paid. Not ranked. Not guaranteed.

WØM Selection is for bars worth discovering and worth returning to. It is not a paid badge and it is not sold as a commercial package.

What it stands for

Atmosphere, feeling, people, experience, memory, social energy and a genuine desire to return.

A place you don’t just visit — a place you come back to.

What it is not

—Not paid: bars cannot buy WØM Selection.
—Not ranked: hospitality is not reduced to a rigid ladder.
—Not guaranteed: visibility and selection remain editorial.

Commercial work can exist around visibility, campaigns, events or partnerships — but the badge itself remains independent.

What we do

World of Mixologists is editorial: we highlight people and moments shaping modern hospitality. When something needs to go further (event, gig, project), Bartendly is the place where it can live and grow.

1) Feature bartenders & bar teams (proper credit)

We repost and highlight bartenders and teams we respect. Always credited. Always tagged. No rankings, no fake awards.

What is Bartendly

Bartendly is a global hospitality platform + a real staffing service arm (private & corporate). Built for hospitality professionals, bars, brands — and clients who want to hire pros.

Bartendly makes hospitality visible and connected worldwide. Your profile, events and opportunities can be discovered across cities — and it becomes easier to connect with other professionals globally.

  • •Global visibility: publish events and be discoverable outside your own network
  • •Jobs & gigs: connect to work opportunities and staffing needs
  • •Private services: get hired for private and corporate events
  • •Networking: easier connections between bartenders, bars and brands globally

Bartendly is both a platform and an operational engine: when a project becomes “real-world” (staffing, timing, delivery), Bartendly can support execution.
